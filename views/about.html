About

I graduated from university in 2014 and started work as a software engineer in a large company. After six years in the city, and a year in the job, I decided I needed a change. So I packed my bags (both of them), quit my job and started a company on the road. Now I get to work on a mix of challenging projects from my clients, and my own projects.


In my technical posts I write about the tecnologies that I use that interest me and how to use them, be that the change over to Angular 2.0 or my experience getting to grips with Ruby on Rails, the odd how-to guide on thing I've just figured out, and a little on personal projects.


My non-technical posts cover everything about my life away from the screen. I travel frequently, and I find this a good way to keep friends and family up to date with things. So expect some travel stories, some exercise, some photodumps and whatever else I feel like posting.
